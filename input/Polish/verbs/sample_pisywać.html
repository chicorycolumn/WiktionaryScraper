Polish[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From pisać +‎ -ywać.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /pʲiˈsɨ.vat͡ɕ/

Verb[edit]

pisywać impf (indeterminate, frequentative, perfective napisać, imperfective pisać)

  1. (transitive) to write occasionally
