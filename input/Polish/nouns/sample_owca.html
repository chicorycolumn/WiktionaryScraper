Etymology[edit]

\n

From Proto-Slavic *ov\xd1\x8cca, from Proto-Indo-European *h\xe2\x82\x83\xc3\xa9wis.\n

\n

Pronunciation[edit]

\n
  • IPA(key): /\xcb\x88\xc9\x94f.t\xcd\xa1sa/
    • \n
  • Audio
    		(file)
\n

Noun[edit]

\n

owca f (diminutive owieczka)\n

\n
  1. sheep, ovine (Ovis gen. et spp.)
    2. \n
  2. ewe (female sheep)
\n
