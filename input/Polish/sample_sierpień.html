Polish[edit]

sierpie\xc5\x84

\n

Etymology[edit]

\n

From sierp (\xe2\x80\x9csickle\xe2\x80\x9d) +‎ -e\xc5\x84, referring to harvesting. Cognate with Czech srpen (\xe2\x80\x9cAugust\xe2\x80\x9d) and Serbo-Croatian srpanj (\xe2\x80\x9cJuly\xe2\x80\x9d).\n
