Polish[edit]

\n
Polish Wikipedia has an article on:
suszarka
Wikipedia pl
\n

Etymology[edit]

\n

From suszy\xc4\x87 +‎ -arka.\n

\n

Pronunciation[edit]

\n
  • IPA(key): /su\xcb\x88\xca\x82ar.ka/
    • \n
  • Audio
    		(file)
    • \n
  • Hyphenation: su\xe2\x80\xa7szar\xe2\x80\xa7ka
    • \n
  • Rhymes: -arka
\n

Noun[edit]

\n

suszarka f\n

\n
  1. dryer (appliance that removes water from something)\n
    suszarka b\xc4\x99bnowa \xe2\x80\x95 tumble dryer
    2. \n
  2. hairdryer
    3. \n
  3. drying rack, drainer, airer
    4. \n
  4. (slang, humorous) radar gun
\n
